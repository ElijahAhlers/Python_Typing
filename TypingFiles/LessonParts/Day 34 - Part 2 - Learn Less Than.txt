Today you will learn the less than symbol.  This symbol is used to indicate the smaller of two values.  For this symbol you will use your k finger to reach down to the , key.  Before you hit the , key you must hold down the shift button on your left hand.  Please practice < < < <.  Good job.  k < k < k < k, k, k, K<, k<, K<, Ten is < than Twenty.  14 < 35.
