Today you will learn the greater than symbol.  This symbol is used to indicate the larger of two values.  For this symbol you will use your l finger to reach down to the . key.  Before you hit the . key you must hold down the shift button on your left hand.  Please practice > > >> >>> >> >.  Good Job.  25 is > than 20.  35 < 74.  (88 - 66) > (22 - 11).
